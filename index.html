Our Story

Founded 1998 · Kerala Wing of ABRSM

"UNNATHA VIDYABHYASA ADHYAPAKA SANGHAM" (UVAS) began functioning in the year 1998. It works as an affiliated organisation of Akhil Bharathiya Rashtriya Shaikshik Mahasangh (ABRSM), New Delhi.

ABRSM is the largest organisation of teachers in the country with a total membership of over fourteen hundred thousand teachers ranging from the pre-primary stage to the university level. It has membership from all states and union territories of the country and is firmly cemented by a nationalistic identity.

ABRSM and UVAS envision a cultural, economic and intellectual progress rooted in 'Dharma' and fostered by the principled stand and moral integrity of each of its members. The organisation has vociferously and adamantly taken a strong stand against any anti-national and sectarian aspect of the curricula; it is intolerant to the practice among certain section of the teachers to spread retrogressive and anti-India ideas into the young minds in colleges and universities.

UVAS is firmly entrenched in the values of cultural nationalism and is duty bound to serve the society and country in a selfless manner.

RK

Is increment due during medical leave applicable under new UGC regulations?

Rajesh Kumar · Assistant Professor · Ernakulam · 5 days ago

Leave RulesUGC
3 replies ▾
🟢 UVAS Official Response

Yes, under UGC Regulations 2018 Clause 9.2, increment is protected during sanctioned medical leave of up to 6 months. Please submit Form ML-3 along with medical certificate to your Principal.

Meena S · Associate Professor · Thrissur

I had the same issue. The official response above is correct — make sure the leave is sanctioned formally, not just approved verbally.

PS

What documents are needed for pension processing after retirement in aided colleges?

Priya Suresh · Professor · Kozhikode · 8 days ago

Pension / RetirementAided College
5 replies ▾
🟢 UVAS Official Response

Documents required: (1) Service Book, (2) NOC from college, (3) Form 6A pension application, (4) Life certificate, (5) Bank passbook copy, (6) Aadhaar & PAN. Submit to DPI office 6 months before retirement date.

AM

Can a self-financing college teacher claim HRA under state rules?

Anoop Mathew · Assistant Professor · Palakkad · 12 days ago

Pay & AllowancesSelf-Financing
2 replies ▾
🟢 UVAS Official Response

HRA for self-financing college teachers is governed by the individual college's management rules, not state government rules directly. However, if the college follows UGC pay scales, HRA should follow UGC norms. Raise this with your management committee formally in writing.
